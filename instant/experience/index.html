Why The Levy Instant Event Portrait Station?

The Ultimate Gift for Your Guests

Your guests receive professional portraits right at the event—a premium service that usually costs a significant amount at a private studio.

Studio Standard Artistry: Our experienced photographer provides expert posing and artistic direction for every session. Whether captured individually, as a couple, or as a full family, your guests receive editorial-grade portraits while they are already styled and dressed at their best.

The Instant & Private Experience:

  • Direct-to-Phone Delivery: Photos are delivered to the guest's phone within minutes of their session via a branded, private gallery.
  • Onsite Editing & AI Retouching (Optional): Portraits are refined instantly for an editorial look. A dedicated technician manages a live process of color grading and AI retouching.
  • Onsite Printing (Optional): Digital portraits become physical 4x6 prints in seconds. These serve as tangible, luxury keepsakes for guests to take home.

A Sophisticated Production & Lasting Legacy

The station serves as a high-end production that elevates the event's atmosphere while providing a permanent, editorial-grade archive.

Tailored Aesthetics: While we offer signature looks, the station is fully adaptable. We work with the host to calibrate lighting and color grading to perfectly match the event's theme and visual identity.

Engaging Event Entertainment: The station acts as an interactive highlight, keeping guests busy and entertained during cocktail hour or the reception. It transforms the event into a sophisticated, fashion-house production.

A Complete Event Legacy: The host will be provided with a master gallery containing every guest and family portrait. This exclusive archive captures your inner circle in a high-fashion studio style, providing a lasting record of your celebration.
