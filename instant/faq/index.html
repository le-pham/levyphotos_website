Frequently Asked Questions

For the Host & Event Planner

1. What are the space and power requirements?

We typically require a 10'x10' footprint and access to a standard 15-amp power outlet. To house our high-speed printer and technical equipment, we also require one sturdy 6ft or 8ft draped table to be provided by the venue or host. If the event is outdoors or in a remote location, please let us know so we can coordinate power solutions.

2. What is the difference between the Base Package and Standard Onsite Editing?

The Base Package is optimized for maximum speed, delivering high-quality images straight from the camera to the guest's device in 20–40 seconds. The Standard Onsite Editing upgrade adds a second team member (a Dedicated Digital Tech) who manually reviews, crops, and applies our "Signature Look" color grading to every single frame. This provides a more curated, polished, and editorial feel, with delivery to the gallery taking approximately 15–20 minutes.

3. How does the "Vogue Upgrade" (AI Retouching) work?

Our AI Retouching utilizes high-speed dedicated servers to apply advanced, editorial-grade facial enhancements in real-time. Unlike basic filters, our system performs precise skin smoothing, blemish removal, eye enhancement, and facial contouring while preserving natural skin texture. This ensures every guest looks like they’ve just stepped off a professional magazine shoot, with finished results delivered to their gallery within 15–20 minutes.

4. What kind of equipment do you use?

We utilize a full studio-grade production stack to ensure the highest reliability and image quality. This includes professional high-resolution mirrorless systems, professional studio strobes with large softbox modifiers, DNP DS40 high-speed dye-sublimation printers (archive quality), dual-path 5G hotspots + Starlink satellite terminals for connectivity, and real-time dedicated GPU servers for AI enhancement.

5. Do you have insurance and backup equipment?

Yes. We operate with a "Zero-Failure" philosophy. We carry redundant backups for every critical component of our station—including backup camera bodies, lenses, studio lighting, and secondary printing units. Additionally, Levy Photography is fully insured with general liability coverage. We are happy to provide a Certificate of Insurance (COI) to your venue upon request.

6. What if my venue has no cell service or Wi-Fi?

This is where we excel. We bring our own high-speed 5G hotspots and Starlink satellite terminals to ensure 100% connectivity, regardless of the venue's infrastructure. We ensure your guests receive their photos even in the most remote locations.

7. How much time do you need for setup and teardown?

Our team arrives 60 to 90 minutes prior to the scheduled start time to ensure lighting is calibrated and the network is stable. Teardown typically takes 45 minutes. This setup time is included and does not count toward your hours of coverage.

8. Will the host receive a master gallery of all images?

Absolutely. While guests receive their individual photos instantly, the host will be provided with a link to a full master gallery containing every portrait taken during the event within 48 hours of the conclusion.

9. Can we access the original, unedited photos?

Yes. While guest galleries feature the polished final results, the host will have full access to all original, unedited high-resolution JPEG captures within their master gallery.

10. Do you provide the RAW files?

To maintain the professional standards and technical integrity of our brand, we provide only finished, high-resolution JPEGs. This ensures every image you receive is a complete work of art that represents our signature quality. The host will receive both the professionally edited files and the original unedited high-resolution JPEGs.

11. Can we add a custom logo or event watermark to the photos?

Yes. We can design a bespoke, elegant overlay or "digital mat" featuring your event logo, date, or monogram to ensure every portrait serves as a branded keepsake of your celebration.

12. Do you provide a backdrop?

Yes. A professional-grade solid backdrop (Studio White, Slate Grey, or Deep Black) can be included upon request. However, for a truly bespoke look, we love collaborating with your florist or event designer to photograph guests against custom floral installs or venue-specific decor.

13. Do you provide props?

Absolutely! We believe high-end shouldn't be boring. We provide a curated selection of high-quality props tailored to your vision. Whether you want sophisticated accessories or fun, themed items, we are happy to coordinate the right props for your event or incorporate any unique items or furniture your team provides.

For Your Guests

1. How do guests find their photos?

We use a seamless AI-powered registration. Guests simply scan a QR code provided at the station and take a quick "check-in selfie" on their device. Our system then uses facial recognition to instantly organize their private gallery containing only their photos.

2. How fast do guests receive their photos?

For the Base Package, digital delivery is near-instant (20–40 seconds). For sessions including Standard Onsite Editing or AI Retouching, we prioritize quality—delivering final, professionally polished portraits to the gallery within 15–20 minutes.

3. Are the private guest galleries secure?

Yes. Our AI facial recognition ensures that each guest only sees the photos they appear in. We prioritize privacy while making the experience frictionless and magical.

4. Can guests share their portraits directly to social media?

Yes! The custom gallery allows for one-click downloading and sharing to Instagram, Facebook, or via text and email, allowing guests to show off their professional portraits in real-time.

5. How does the AI Retouching work?

Our "Vogue Upgrade" uses a dedicated server to apply editorial-grade retouching (skin smoothing, eye enhancement, and blemish removal) to every photo in real-time, ensuring guests look their absolute best.

6. How do guests get their physical prints?

If the host has included Onsite Dye-Sub Printing, guests can simply select their favorite portrait from their private digital gallery and tap the "Print" button. High-quality 4x6 prints will be sent to our station and ready for pickup in seconds.
